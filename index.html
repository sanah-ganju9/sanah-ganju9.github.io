Memory Lane

Along his beautiful journey of 11 years, he had managed to touch many lives. People who knew him well, people who had met him on few occasions, people who had heard so much about him from me and even people who had heard so much about him from other people. Moona was a superstar and he never lacked love. I hope wherever you are, there are plenty of pappis and hugs! I will love you and miss you, always.
-In loving memory of Moona Cat (26 Jan 2003- 7 May 2014)
